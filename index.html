Prosthetic Specialist

Mr. Sugath Laggoda

Prosthesist & Orthotist

Specialized and highly experienced in advanced prosthetic solutions, limb restorations, and custom orthotic alignments. Offering world-class prosthetic care across a comprehensive range of amputation levels.

Prosthetic Specializations
  • • Hip disarticulation: Amputation of the entire lower limb at the hip joint.
  • • Transfemoral: Amputation across the thigh bone (above knee level).
  • • Knee disarticulation: Amputation through the knee joint.
  • • Below knee: Amputation below the knee joint.
  • • Ankle disarticulation: Amputation through the ankle joint.
  • • Partial foot: Amputation of a portion of the foot structure.

Our Objectives

Improve Quality of Life

To provide necessary facilities and support to persons with disabilities in order to improve their quality of life through access to essential devices and resources.

Education, Training & Rehabilitation

To implement programs related to education, training, rehabilitation, self-employment, and social empowerment of persons with disabilities.

Promote Awareness & Rights

To promote awareness and protect the rights of persons with disabilities within society, ensuring they are heard, valued, and respected.

Social Integration & Equal Opportunity

To encourage social integration and ensure equal opportunities for persons with disabilities in every sector of society.

Independent Living

To lead an independent life for persons with disabilities in the fields of culture, education, economics, recreation, and entertainment.

Government Policy Alignment

Everything we do aligns with the National Human Policy of Sri Lanka for persons with disabilities, ensuring our work is lawful, ethical, and impactful.
